The Ball
========

Each size class uses its own ball which is at least 50% white.
The remaining colors of the ball are undefined but should not contain significant amounts of cyan, magenta, field green or white.

#. KidSize: Orange Ball with a diameter of approx.
   10cm FIFA size 1 ball (diameter approx. 13cm, weight approx. 150g).
#. TeenSize: FIFA size 3 ball, at least 50% orange.
#. AdultSize: FIFA size 5 ball, at least 50% orange.
